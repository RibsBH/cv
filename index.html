Steve Vine

Founder and Director of Blue Ochre Design.

During my early career as a computer programmer, my favourite part of the job was designing the screens for the programs we created. Later moving into software project management, the highlight of my week was putting together a PowerPoint presentation to present to management or my team. You never know where life is going to take you and it was a surprise to everyone, including me, when I announced I was going to open a bakery in a remote town in Far Western NSW. Throughout the 14 years of operating our bakery franchise, amongst baking bread by hand every day, any opportunity to create a marketing poster or Facebook post was met with personal glee, so it's no surprise that about 3 years ago, I had an epiphany that I'm actually a designer in disguise.Ripping the disguise off, I completed a Diploma of Graphic Design and loved it, so here I am ready to continue what I've been doing for years, but now in the wild. Hello, my name is Steve Vine and I'm a Graphic Designer. So if you'd like someone passionate about design with a knowledge of operating and marketing a small business, I'm your man. I know from my experience, marketing your business takes a back seat when you're busy with the day to day. Marketing effectively means growth, so get in touch and let's have a chat about how I can take the marketing load off your shoulders and help you grow your business, leaving you to do what you do best.
